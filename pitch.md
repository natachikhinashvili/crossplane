A hands-on Crossplane v2 course for Kubernetes engineers who want to build real platform APIs without spending a cent on cloud. You'll write XRDs and Compositions, work with composition functions, and ship a complete self-service WebApp API — one custom resource that provisions networking, storage, IAM, a Postgres database, and a running application your developers can curl. Everything runs locally on LocalStack and kind. Along the way you'll learn when Crossplane beats Terraform and when it doesn't. By the end, you'll have the foundation to bring Crossplane into your team with confidence
